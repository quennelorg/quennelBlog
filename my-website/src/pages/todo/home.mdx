### MongoDB: No server chosen by ReadPreferenceServerSelector
将springboot到放到docker里，然后ServerDescription{address=localhost:27017, type=UNKNOWN, state=CONNECTING}]，连接出错，如果将localhost改成远程的地址则没有问题
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/29718933/mongodb-no-server-chosen-by-readpreferenceserverselector

https://dev.to/docker/run-x86-containers-on-apple-mac-m1-with-rosetta-2-417a
